Burguer Maker

Burger Maker is my first independent game, a burger-making simulator where the difficulty increases as your score grows. It features two modes: a single-player mode where you compete for a spot on the global rankings, and a competitive mode where two players can challenge each other on the same iPad to see who can make burgers the fastest and most accurately. The game also supports multitouch, allowing it to identify which player is making each touch during gameplay.
